About Me

Steven Kurniawan is a self taught photographer and videographer, based in Texas and originally from Queens, New York. Consumed and infatuated by the lively nature of the city, and inspiration from the people, the environments, and the personalities, his style of work elevates everyday moments into a visual masterpiece that entangles colors, compositions, and subjects into a focal point and a subject of conversation. He believes that every visual image captured is a story of what was and a reflection to evoke a sense of emotions, a heightened feeling, and a captured moment in time. Every photo and film should narrate a story, whether you are the subject or you are the interpreter. His goal is to make the ordinary moments into something you can look back on for a lifetime.

Over the years, Steven has been engulfed by many different ventures and operated at many different capacities in different industries and roles. He has a Bachelor's degree in Economics and a Master's degree in Data Analytics and Applied Social Research. He has worked in academia, tech, media, sports, and real estate sectors at different capacities as a Production and Social Media Intern, a Data Analyst, and a Research and Analytics Manager. He even had a stint as an adjunct professor teaching statistics. Within the many different roles and industries, it exposed him to many different business practices, operational systems, and the inner workings of how important teams and structures are at any capacity. The skills he learned and continued to develops taught him about more than just the tangible skills, but the workings of being a servant and leader at the same capacity. He has formulated his skillsets in marketing, sales, and analytics, but his main goal applies at any role he takes on, which is being able to craft a story and being client and user centric. The goal is not business, but developing relationships along the way, and within his roles he has been able to attentively listen to the client's wants and objectives, and executing ideas into life.

The mission of this business is based off a greater calling and purpose to advancing God's Kingdom. The central focus point is not about himself, but pointing it back to the one who has radically changed his heart and saved his life. His faith is pertinent to how Steven operates with people, with how he conducts his business, and how he lives his life. The main goal is to reflect Christ and to remind and disseminate to everyone he encounters and impacts that we are created in God's image and that Jesus is the son of God who died on the cross and defeated death to save humanity to justify us with the Father, so that we can know and expierence pure and righteous love. Every person is important and every story matters to Jesus, and Steven is in full pursuit of fulfilling the great commission one person at a time, one conversation at a time, and one snapshot at a time.
